Canva Project: Explaining Past Life Regression

I designed an Instagram post in Canva for my father’s account on the topic “What is PLR (Past Life Regression)?”. The post explains PLR as a gentle hypnotherapy method where a person is guided into a relaxed state to explore what are believed to be memories from past lives, often for personal insight or healing. Through this project, I practised turning a complex spiritual and psychological idea into a clear, visually engaging design for social media.
